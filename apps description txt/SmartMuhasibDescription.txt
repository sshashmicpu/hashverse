Smart Muhasib – Complete Islamic Self-Accountability Tracker
Track Salah, Goals, Tasbeeh & More – All in One Modern App

Why Smart Muhasib is the Best App for Your Spiritual & Personal Growth:

✅ Complete Salah Tracker
Easily mark each prayer as "Prayed," "On Time," and even "Nafl." The app automatically calculates your daily score, so you always know where you stand. (Optional: hide Nafl with a single toggle.)

✅ Custom Goals
Create your own targets using checkboxes, counters, or text entries. Whether it’s Quran recitation, daily Duas, or any habit, Smart Muhasib adapts to your needs.

✅ Smart Tasbeeh Counter
- Full-screen mode lets you tap anywhere to count.
- Set a target and the app will beep when you complete a round, automatically tracking loops.
- Auto Mode: Tap at your natural pace for 20 seconds – the app learns your speed and starts counting automatically! Pause, resume, or re-train anytime.

✅ Multi-User Support
Add family members, switch between users, copy goals from one user to another, and compare weekly or monthly performance. Perfect for the whole family.

✅ Detailed Reports
View your progress for today, this week, this month, or the whole year. The app highlights your best and weakest goals, helping you focus on improvement.

✅ Offline & Private
All your data is stored locally in your browser – no internet, no servers, no privacy concerns.

✅ Beautiful Islamic Theme
Choose between Light, Dark, or specially designed Islamic theme with warm, spiritual colors and gold accents.

✅ Data Backup & Restore
- Export all data as a JSON file or copy it as text to save safely.
- Import previously saved data to restore your full history.
- Reset everything with one click if needed.

✅ Streak Tracking
Monitor your longest streak based on any prayer (choose Fajr, Zohar, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, or Tahajjud). Stay motivated to maintain consistency.

✅ Mood Logging
Record your daily mood to reflect on your emotional and spiritual journey over time.

✅ Tasbeeh Templates
Quick-load popular Tasbeehs like SubhanAllah (33), Alhamdulillah (33), Allahu Akbar (34), Durood Sharif (100), Istighfar (100), and more.
Plus, create your own custom templates with a name and target – or even a "Without Name" option.

✅ History & Review
View the last 10 days of performance at a glance – each day's score, completion count, and mood.

✅ Intuitive Navigation
Six main tabs (Home, Goals, Tasbeeh, Users, Reports, Settings) make it easy to switch between different parts of the app.
