Maqsood-ul-Quran App ✨

​"Understand the Holy Quran with Ease."

​Maqsood-ul-Quran is a smart digital Quran reader designed to make reading and understanding the Holy Quran simple for everyone. It features the beautiful Al-Kitab-ur-Rahman translation, which is written in very easy language.

​📖 About the Translation:

​Easy to Read: The translation is written in simple words so children and adults can understand it easily.

​Pure Message: It focuses on the true message of the Quran, free from any specific group or sect.

​Beautiful Look: The text and graphics are designed to be clean and clear for a better reading experience.

​🌟 Smart App Features:

​This app is built with advanced tools to help you stay focused on your recitation:

​Smart Memory: The app automatically remembers the last page you were reading. When you open it again, it starts exactly where you left off.

​Auto-Scroll: You can set the page to move down automatically (like a teleprompter) so you can read without touching the screen.

​Time Tracker: See exactly how many minutes or hours you spent reading the Quran today.

​Dark & Dim Modes: Switch to Dark Mode or Dim Light to protect your eyes when reading at night.

​Quick Navigation: Use the "Go To" button or the slider to jump to any page or Surah instantly.

​Works Offline: You don't need the internet to read. Open the app and start your journey anywhere.

​🛠 Why this App is Better:

​Clean Design: No distracting ads or complicated menus.

​Full Screen Mode: Hide all buttons with one tap to focus only on the Quranic text.

​High Quality: Every word is sharp and easy to read, even on small screens.

​Maqsood-ul-Quran – Bringing the message of the Quran to every heart.
