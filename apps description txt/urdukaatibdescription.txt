Urdu Kaatib: The Professional Urdu & Arabic Desktop Publisher ✍️✨

​Experience the power of a desktop publishing suite on your mobile! Urdu Kaatib is the first-of-its-kind tool designed for professional Urdu and Arabic typesetting. Whether you are creating Islamic posters, writing articles, or managing complex Urdu data, this app is your ultimate companion.

​🔥 Exclusive Advanced Features:

​🖋️ Multi-Font Typography: Choose from professional fonts like Jameel Noori Nastaliq, Amiri (Arabic), Lateef, and more. Perfect for achieving that authentic "InPage" look.

​🤖 Smart Auto-Arabic Detection: Write Urdu and Arabic together! The app automatically identifies Arabic text and applies specialized fonts to keep the script accurate and beautiful.

​📂 Dynamic Data Management: * Save/Load Project: Never lose your work. Save your current session and resume whenever you want. 

​Import/Export Files: Move your projects as data files between devices or share them with other developers.

​📄 Professional Output: * Export to PDF: Convert your entire work into a high-quality PDF document instantly. 

​Image Conversion: Turn your text layouts into clear visuals for social media.

​🎨 Full Creative Control (Side Toolbar):

​🌈 Color Customization: * Text Color: Change the color of your words with a professional color picker. 

​Background Color: Set the perfect canvas color for your document.

​Arabic Color: Give Arabic text a different color automatically for better highlighting.

​Font Size Slider: Adjust text size from 1 to 100 with precision.
