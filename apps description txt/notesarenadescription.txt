Notes Arena: Premium Edition 🚀

​The Smartest Way to Save Your Thoughts.

​Notes Arena is a powerful digital notebook designed to keep your notes organized, safe, and stylish. Whether you are writing a quick reminder, a shopping list, or a long story, this app makes it easy for you.

​🌟 Best Features:

​Cloud Backup: Login with Google to save your notes. Your data stays safe even if you change your phone.

​Two Types of Notes: Create a regular Text Note or a Checklist (To-Do List) for your daily tasks.

​Urdu & English Friendly: The app automatically knows if you are typing in Urdu or English and fixes the text direction for you.

​Built-in Calculator: Need to do some math while writing? Use the floating calculator right inside the app!

​Find Words Fast: Search for any specific word inside your note, and the app will highlight it for you.

​🎨 Make it Yours:

​6 Beautiful Themes: Change colors and backgrounds like "Neon Cyber" or "Forest Mist" to match your mood.

​Font Switcher: Pick from 4 different font styles to make your text look professional.

​Modern Design: A clean, futuristic look that is very easy to use on mobile.

​🛠 Extra Tools:

​Recycle Bin: Deleted a note by mistake? Don’t worry! You can easily restore it from the Bin.

​Save & Share: Save your notes as TXT files or share them directly to WhatsApp with one click.

​Pin Important Notes: Keep your most important tasks at the very top of the list.

​Character Counter: See exactly how many letters you have written in real-time.
