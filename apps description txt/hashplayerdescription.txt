HASH PLAYER  🚀

​"More than a Player—It’s a Vibe."

​Hash Player is a high-tech music and video player designed for people who love aesthetic vibes. Created by Syed Sakhir Hashmi, this app lets you change your music into something special with just one tap.

​🌟 Unique Features (Only in Hash Player):

​S+R Mode (Slowed + Reverb): This is the best feature! It instantly slows down your music and adds a deep "echo" effect. It makes every song sound like a relaxing Lofi masterpiece.

​Background Vibe Engine: You can play two things at once! For example, play your favorite song and add a "Rain Sound" or "Chill Beat" in the background. You can control the volume of both separately.

​Neural Sound Effects: Use the FX Panel to boost the Bass, make the Vocals crystal clear, or add a cool Echo like you are in a big hall.

​🎨 Beautiful Design:

​7 Cool Themes: Change the look of the app with futuristic backgrounds like "Neon Cyber" or "Space Nebula."

​Dancing Visualizers: Watch the colorful bars and circles move perfectly with the beat of your music.

​Modern Glass Look: A clean, stylish design with blur effects that looks premium on your phone.

​Urdu Font Support: Beautiful fonts (Amiri & Nastaliq) that make Urdu song names look professional.

​🛠 Powerful Tools:

​Speed & Pitch Control: Make your music faster or slower, and change the voice from deep to high-pitched.

​Smart Memory: The app remembers your songs, volume, and theme settings even if you close it.

​Sleep Timer: Set a timer so the music stops automatically while you sleep.

​Brightness (Lux) Control: Easily change the screen brightness inside the app.

​Gesture Swipe: Swipe on the screen to change tracks or adjust the volume quickly.

​🔐 Extra Perks:

​Plays Audio & Video: No need for two apps. Watch videos or listen to MP3s in the same place.

​Recycle Bin: Deleted a track by mistake? You can easily manage your library.

​Exit Alert: The app asks before closing so your music doesn't stop by accident.

​Hash Player – Create your own atmosphere and enjoy music like never before.
